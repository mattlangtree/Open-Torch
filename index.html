Open Torch by Abhi Beckert

Torch app for iPhone 4

Open Torch is a simple torch app for iPhone 4.

It does not contain any on/off button, or strobe feature, or sound effects, or banner ads. It will not harass you to pay for a "pro" version.

Usage

Open the app to turn on the torch. Close it to turn off the torch. Simple!

Installation

  • Open the project in Xcode 4
  • Select your iPhone as the target
  • Build and run
